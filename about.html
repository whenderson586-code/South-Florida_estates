About Our Brokerage

Personal representation backed by real market experience.

South Florida Estates was created to provide buyers, sellers, landlords and investors with straightforward guidance and responsive service throughout one of the country's most dynamic real estate markets.

Led by licensed real estate broker William Henderson, the brokerage brings decades of business, finance, mortgage and real estate experience to every transaction. Our approach combines detailed market knowledge with clear communication, careful negotiation and personal accountability.
